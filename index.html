Happy Mothers Day

Date: 09/29/2020 by Naomi Benjamin


Mommy, I’m-... Stranded. With a chest full of Urgency. Wasted. No boat could sail for me now. But the incessant need to see you, Be with you, cry with you, Was a trying companion to a breaking heart.

Mommy, You don’t deserve to leave life alone. You don’t deserve to return to your Father, With two empty hands and a heavy heart. But you sound stronger today. Hopeful, and my heart took on that strength. Mommy I-... I felt it when you left before the phone rang. I felt the shallow void run deep As toned, sorrowful condolences showed you lifeless on the bed.

But It's Not So Happy

Date: 09/30/2020 by Naomi Benjamin


You, another bag in luggage reclaim. Me, another failure. Another drop of loss in the pit of plagued bodies.

Mommy, I’m-... Home. With a chest full of Guilt. I deserved it. No boat could sail for me now.

Since I didn't know what to write, the posts together are a poem I I wrote for a Creative Writing course I did in second semester of my first year. Don't even know if this will actually get read but if yeah, hope you like it. Kinda sad I know, but its the reality some people face going through this whole pandemic that has literally just turned peoples lives over...enough sad stuff. Have a good day!
